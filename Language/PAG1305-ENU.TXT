N1305-C107-P8629-A1033-L999:Invoice Discount Amount
N1305-C107-P8631-A1033-L999:Specifies a discount amount that is deducted from the value in the Total Incl. VAT field. You can enter or change the amount manually. If you have set up invoice discounts, the specified percentage is shown in the Invoice Discount % field if the criteria are met. The calculated discount amount is then inserted in the Invoice Discount Amount field.
N1305-C108-P8629-A1033-L999:Customer Invoice Discount %
N1305-C108-P8631-A1033-L999:Specifies a discount percentage that is granted if the criteria are met that you have set up for invoice discounts. The calculated discount amount is inserted in the Invoice Discount Amount field, but you can change it manually.

N1305-C12-P8631-A1033-L999:Specifies the price for one unit on the sales line. By default, the field is filled with the value in the Unit Price field on the item or resource card.

N1305-C2-P8629-A1033-L999:Total VAT
N1305-C2-P8631-A1033-L999:Specifies the sum of VAT amounts on all lines in the document. Note that amounts on document lines are shown with or without VAT depending on what you selected in the Prices Including VAT field on the customer or vendor card.

N1305-C3-P8631-A1033-L999:Specifies the sum of the value in the Line Amount Incl. VAT field on all lines in the document minus any discount amount in the Invoice Discount Amount field.
